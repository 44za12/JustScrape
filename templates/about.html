About


A simple JustDial Scrapper made using python, re, flask. This tool scrapes results from a JustDial URL of your choice.

On provision of a valid Just Dial URL the app returns a CSV file with Name, Phone, Rating, Address, Location, City and Image of every listing.

In case you face any problems, feel free to contact using the contact page.
